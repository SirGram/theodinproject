Concealed in the Doppelganger Closet, a villainous creation spawned replicas indistinguishable from their counterparts. The malefactor aimed to infiltrate society, replacing key figures with identical impostors, sowing chaos and subverting trust from within.

Fake-News Spreader

Fusion Energy Extractor

Weather Manipulator
